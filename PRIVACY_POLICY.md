# Hex Zero Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 27, 2025_

## Introduction

Hex Zero ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile puzzle game, Hex Zero.

## Information We Collect

### Information We Do NOT Collect

Hex Zero is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Photos, videos, or camera data
- Microphone or audio data
- Contact lists
- Health or fitness data
- Financial information
- Browsing history

### Game Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

- Game progress and save states
- High scores and achievements
- Game settings and preferences
- Time spent playing

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

## How We Use Information

Since Hex Zero does not collect personal information, we do not use, share, or sell any user data. All game data remains on your device for the sole purpose of providing you with a continuous gaming experience.

## Third-Party Services

Hex Zero does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The game operates entirely offline once downloaded.

## Network Access

The INTERNET permission in our Android app is required only for:

- Downloading the app from the app store
- Potential future app updates through the app store

The game itself does not make any network requests during gameplay.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- Data is protected by your device's security measures
- We cannot access your game data
- Data is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Children's Privacy

Hex Zero is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- All data is stored locally on your device
- You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
- You can reset game progress through the app settings

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
